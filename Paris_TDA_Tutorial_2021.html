DS3-2020, Topological Data Analysis: Mapper, Persistence and Applications Tutorial

Welcome on the webpage of our TDA tutorial. Over here you will find some basic material and link to code. If you have any questions, please feel free to drop me a line! During our session we will use jupyter-notebook and R. Please make sure that you have it installed prior to the tutorial (we have very little time and a lot of ground to cover!). Here are the libraries you need to install on Python. In R, you need BallMapper which can be obtained by typing install.packages("BallMapper") into your R terminal. This tutorial will be presented by me and Vincent Rouvreau from Inria Saclay. You will get a separated hello email message from us!
