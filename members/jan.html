Jan Felix Senge

I am a PhD student from the University in Bremen at the Institute for Algebra, Geometry, Topology and their Applications (ALTA) with Prof. Dr. Dmitry Feichtner-Kozlov as advisor. I am working Paweł Dłotko at the Dioscuri Center for TDA in Applied Topology and Topological Data Analysis (TDA).
My focus lies in descriptors/signatures, kernel-methods, and statistical summaries for persistent homology. The applications are currently surface roughness, medical imagery - bone cracks, and economy.
